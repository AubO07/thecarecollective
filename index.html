The Care Collective

Dear Rt Hon Peter Kyle MP,

Britain’s unpaid carers prop up our health & social-care system every single day – yet they remain invisible in policymaking and in tech innovation. The Care Collective is our answer: a secure Digital Care ID plus an AI-supported platform that connects carers to services, unlocks creative contribution, and saves the Treasury billions through smarter welfare spend.

You’ve said you want social care fixed, creativity championed and AI harnessed for public good. We couldn’t agree more. Let’s prototype this together – starting with the people who already “get the job done” for free.

With gratitude & urgency,
Aubrey Ghose
