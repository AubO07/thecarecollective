Open Letter ~ Peter Kyle MP

Britain’s unpaid carers save the Treasury over £162 billion every year yet remain overlooked by policy and tech innovation.

The Care Collective is our answer: a secure Digital ID & support platform that connects carers, unlocks contribution and transforms lives.

Read the letter ↓

Dear Peter,

We write on behalf of Britain’s 12 million unpaid carers. Every day they provide vital, uncompensated support to family and friends, saving the public purse more than the annual NHS wage bill, yet they remain largely invisible to policy-makers and digital-service designers.

The Care Collective proposes a single, secure Carer ID — built on open standards and NHS-level privacy — that lets carers prove their role once and unlock tailored benefits, flexible-work arrangements and micro-payments wherever they live. Our first prototype will be live in June 2025 and the full public changelog sits on GitHub.

With your department’s partnership we can:

  1. Pilot Carer ID in three Integrated Care Systems.
  2. Integrate with the NHS App to surface personalised respite services.
  3. Export the template across Commonwealth partners, scaling British tech leadership and soft power.

We would welcome the opportunity to brief you and Treasury colleagues ahead of the NHS-AI allocation.

Yours sincerely,
The Care Collective team
